Snorkeling in the Mangrove at JungutBatu Lembongan

Duration : about 45 minutes

One of the snorkeling locations in Nusa Lembongan is in a location near to the mangrove point / mangrove area, we provide snorkeling tours in only 1 location at very affordable price (no need for a whole day – especially if your stay is super limited in Nusa Lembongan). Start the day with a fantastic underwater adventure, and seeing lots of exotic fish and colorful algae. Snorkeling equipment and life jackets have been provided, you only need to bring a swimsuit and extra clothes, sunblock and an underwater camera if you want to take pictures while snorkeling. Very safe even for those who are an expert of swimming, there will be a guide to taking care of you. This tour is not recommended for those who have heart complaint and other chronic health problems.

Leave your valuables at hotel if possible. Bring some clean clothes to wear after the visit to the beach farm (the one that you wear will get some dirt so you’ll need to change to the clean one). Tour may be operated by a multi-lingual guide. Not recommended for participants with heart complaints or other serious medical conditions. For under 15 years old, they need to be accompanied by an adult who will be responsible for them.

PRICE FOR THIS TOUR :

Rp. 175.000. (EXCLUDE TOUR GUIDE IN ENGLISH IF NEEDED, IT IS OK WITHOUT GUIDE)

GUIDE FEE IS ABOUT Rp. 200.000
