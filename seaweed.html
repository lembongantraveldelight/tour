Seaweed Farming Private Tour, Nusa Lembongan

Duration : about 2.5 – 3 hours tour

Seaweed Farming tour can be done during the low tide. This tour has a high traditional value because in practice, the stages of activities from preparation, planting, maintenance to harvesting, and cultivation are passed on from one generation to the next. Visitors get a rare opportunity to experience the daily life of seaweed farming villagers.

Highlights :

  • Visiting local village in the Mangrove Point, JunggutBatu, Nusa Lembongan
  • Learning about the economy and daily life planting seaweed by the villagers
  • Round-trip transport provided – by motorcycle (from and to your hotel)
  • Going out to the beach farm to get the seaweed, plant seaweed and harvest seaweed by small boat (you can see the Mount Agung View from a far on the boat on the way to the seaweed beach farm)

What's included :

  • Pick up and drop off from your hotel by motorcycle (by local staffs)
  • Entrances Fees
  • Welcome Drink
  • All taxes, fuel surcharges and service fees included

What's excluded :

  • Experienced Tour Guide (in English)
  • Flights to Bali and Airport Taxes
  • Fast Boat to Nusa Lembongan from Sanur
  • Visa (for international visitors)
  • Personal Expenses

Leave your valuables at hotel if possible. Bring some clean clothes to wear after the visit to the beach farm (the one that you wear will get some dirt so you’ll need to change to the clean one). Tour may be operated by a multi-lingual guide. Not recommended for participants with heart complaints or other serious medical conditions. For under 15 years old, they need to be accompanied by an adult who will be responsible for them.
