We are a tour operator that sells multiple tours with destinations across Nusa Lembongan, Bali. Our goals are to let people discover the beauty of Nusa Lembongan Island and it’s nature, and we would love to support local tourism provided by local villagers.

Our Tour Services

SEAWEED FARMING PRIVATE TOUR, NUSA LEMBONGAN BALI
Duration : about 2.5 – 3 hours tour.

Seaweed Farming tour can be done during the low tide. This tour has a high traditional value because in practice, the stages of activities from preparation, planting, maintenance to harvesting, and cultivation are passed on from one generation to the next. Visitors get a rare opportunity to experience the daily life of seaweed farming villagers.

Duration : about 45 minutes.

Start the day with a fantastic underwater adventure, and seeing lots of exotic fish and colorful algae. Snorkeling equipment and life jackets have been provided, you only need to bring a swimsuit and extra clothes, sunblock and an underwater camera if you want to take pictures while snorkeling.
