East Nusa Penida Island Private Tour with a Car

Duration : about 5 - 7 hours

Stunning white beaches, blue beach water, limestone rocks and palm trees are dream travel destinations. NUSA PENIDA is without a doubt one of the things you must visit in Bali. The island is less developed, so it is not recommended to bring motorbikes here as the roads are narrow and there are lots of other vehicles, as well as lots of bumpy road obstacles.

The east coast is a bit more untouched.

In this east coast tour, you will visit:

  • DIAMOND BEACH
  • ATUH BEACH
  • THOUSAND ISLANDS
  • TREE HOUSE (information: if you plan to take pictures in here it's not free, you will need to pay an extra 75,000 rupiah per pax at the destination (it is regulation from Tree House, not us)).

Drivers speak Indonesian, very nice local Balinese, but if you don't speak Indonesian and are willing to get a guide, it will be a separate cost, but if just need someone to take you to your destination safely, and you need someone to take your picture, our staff can definitely do the service. It is suitable for those of you who don't like being disturbed by noisy drivers, no unnecessary chatter.
